I made a smart menu for a cafe in Dubai. Instead of just seeing the price, you can find out if the food is healthy just by asking.

How does it work?
You find something like "Chicken Wrap," and my project uses a smart AI (like a digital nutritionist) to find all the facts—like how much salt or sugar is inside.

Why does it matter?
Because people in Dubai come from everywhere, I made sure the AI translates the health info into Hindi and Arabic. Now, even if someone doesn't speak English well, they can understand exactly what they are eating!
